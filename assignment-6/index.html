Chicken, Sausage, Potates, and Veggie Meal

Ingredients:

  • 4 Large - Sausage Links *
  • 4 - Chicken Breasts
  • 6 - Bell Peppers
    • Sliced
  • 1 Small - Red Onion
    • Sliced
  • 6 Large - Gold Potatoes
    • Diced
  • 31/2 Tablespoons - Olive Oil
    • 3 Separate Tablespoons
  • 2 Teaspoons - Italian Herbs
  • 2 Teaspoons - Salt
  • To Taste - Black Pepper

* Flavor up to personal taste


Cooking Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F or 230°C
  2. Heat one tablespoon of oil in skillet over medium heat. Then cook sausage links until browned or about 3 minutes per side
    • Pierce the sausges in a few places to allow some fats and juices to leak out
  3. After sausages are done and cooled enough to handle cut them into 2 inch slices and return to skillet
  4. Add the sliced onions and peppers, the diced potatoes, and the sausages along with the oil and juices from the skillet into a large mixing bowl
  5. Sprinkle on the salt, pepper, italian herbs, and the another tablespoon of olive oil
  6. Coat roasting pan with the last tablespoon and a 1/2 of oil and add chicken breasts
    • Evenly space chicken
  7. Mix ingredients with your hands until all ingredients are coated in oil and transfer to roasting pan on top of chicken breasts
  8. Place in preheated oven for about an hour until the chicken is cooked through and everything is carmalized. Internal chicken temperature should be 165°F or 74°C

Enjoy!
