How to Plan a Trip

Instructable

What is needed:

  • Computer or phone
  • Map software
  • Notepad
  • Pencil

Step 1:

  • Make a list of the places you would like to visit
    • Think about the things you would want to do at the location including resturants and other points of interest
    • Consider how you'll get around at the location
      • Taxis
      • Buses
      • Trains
      • Walking
      • Etc

  • (Optional but recommended)

    Use the satellite view in Google Maps to get a feel for the area and see what the area you want to visit looks like

Step 2:

  • Decide how long the trip will be to help with planning and budgeting

Step 3:

  • Book your flights, accommodations, and anything else you need to schedule ahead of time

Step 4:

  • Plan your day to day activities
    • Check hours, prices, and look for tips from locals
    • Create an itinerary for each day

Step 5:

  • Diligently pack what you need remembering to:
    • Check weather
    • Have clothes needed for special activities that are planned
    • Don't forget nessecities like toiletries, chargers, and documents
